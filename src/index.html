Translations: русский

Project

I was impressed by Harry Stevens's article at Washington Post on outbreaks like COVID-19. His work was clear and easy to understand, but I wanted to be able to control the parameters of such simulation. That's why I made this project.

Warning!

This simulation is not an accurate model of how real diseases spread through a population.

Rules

Rules are simple:

  • There is a restricted territory.
  • People are represented by circles.
  • Some of the population are self-isolating while others roam freely.
  • At the beginning of the simulation part of the population is infected.
  • Infected person spreads the infection to a healthy individual on contact with a set probability.
  • After some time infected recovers and acquires immunity. Now they could neither get infected again nor infect others.
  • Also infected could die with a set probability. This is called a case fatality rate.
